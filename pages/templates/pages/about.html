About Sawacom

Sawacom is your friendly, accessible mental health AI assistant — built to listen, guide, and support your emotional wellbeing when you need it most. Whether you're feeling anxious, overwhelmed, or simply need someone to talk to, Sawacom is here 24/7 to provide a compassionate ear and helpful guidance.

Our Mission

We believe mental health support should be safe, stigma-free, and always available. Our mission is to harness the power of AI to make emotional wellbeing more approachable and personalized for everyone — no matter where you are in your journey.

How It Works

Sawacom uses advanced natural language understanding to engage in meaningful conversations. It can help you reflect on your emotions, track your mental health goals, and even connect you with professional therapists when needed.

Privacy First

All conversations are anonymous and encrypted. We do not store personal data without consent, and you're always in control of your privacy. Learn more in our Privacy Policy.
