Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Sawacom (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use our mental health AI assistant service.

2. Anonymous Usage vs. Member Accounts

  • Anonymous Usage: By default, Sawacom provides anonymous access where no personal information is stored permanently. Your interactions are not linked to an identity, and all session data is cleared when your session ends.
  • Member Accounts: If you choose to create an account, you can save your conversation history, track your mental health progress over time, set personal preferences, and access additional features. Your account data is stored securely and can be managed in your profile settings.

3. Information We Collect

  • 3.1 Information You Provide: When you sign up, chat, or book appointments, we collect information such as your name, email address, age range, messages, and any feedback you provide.
  • 3.2 Automatically Collected Information: We may collect usage data (pages visited, features used), device information (browser type, operating system), and cookies to improve your experience.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Personalize your AI interactions
  • Send you important updates and promotional information (with your consent)
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • Service providers (hosting, analytics) under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Third-party therapists or professionals only with your explicit consent

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit and at rest, to protect your data. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us to request removal.

10. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance functionality and analyze site traffic. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through our app or via email, and update the “Last Updated” date above.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please get in touch.
